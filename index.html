How It Works

Pictures

Send us pictures to being the process. We start by inspecting the damage to determine the extent of the issue and whether the wheel is suitable for repair. If a wheel is too severely damaged, we will recommend a replacement.

Preparation

Once we determine that the wheel is repairable, we prepare the surface by cleaning and removing debris or dirt. We also remove the tire if necessary.

Repair

Depending on the type of damage, we use specialized tools and techniques to repair the wheel.

Refinishing

After the repair is complete, we apply paint to restore the wheel’s appearance. We match the original color as closely as possible or provide a custom finish if desired.

Cure Time

We fully cure the rim leaving the vehicle ready to drive. We recommend no car wash for 5 days or any time mounting or dismounting for at least 30 days.
